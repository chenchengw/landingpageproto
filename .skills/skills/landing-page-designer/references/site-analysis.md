OPSA — AI-Powered Sales Assistant for Wholesalers & Manufacturers

Opsa (logo) | Products | Automations | FAQ | Sign in | Talk to founders

**Hero:**
- Badge: "Automate Revenue!" (green pill)

- Subtext: "Transform your sales team with AI-powered sales assistants that handle quotes, order tracking, and customer inquiries around the clock so your team can focus on high-value relationships."

**Footer:**

### Design Observations
- Light, clean, lots of whitespace
- Serif headlines (dark navy/blue) + sans-serif body
- Green accents for badges, orange accents for product cards
- UI mockup cards as primary visuals (no photography)
- Standard B2B SaaS template feel

## InstaLILY Site (instalily.ai) — Captured March 2026

### Page Title
AI Teammates for the Physical Goods Economy | InstaLILY™

### Navigation
INSTA LILY (logo) | HOME | TEAM | NEWS | AI DISPATCH | CAREERS | Hire InstaWorkers™
Persistent blue top bar: "InstaLILY's Unique Architecture featured in Google DeepMind →"

### Section-by-Section Breakdown

**Hero (full-bleed image background):**
- Headline: "Don't slow down. Sell faster."
- Subtext: "AI that turns complex work into revenue. Built for teams in the physical economy."
- Background: Overhead photo of people working with papers/documents at a table
- Dark overlay on image for text readability

**Why Now Section (light background):**
- Label: "For two hundred years, the physical economy has worked the same way."
- Numbered timeline: 01 Paper catalogs → 02 Phone calls → 03 PDFs → 04 Emails → 05 Spreadsheets → 06 Workarounds
- Statement: "Work didn't get modernized. It just got documented."
- "Paper became PDFs. Calls became emails. Work stayed manual. That's the bottleneck."

**Product Section (dark background):**
- Title: "One company brain. Many teammates."
- InstaBrain SLM™: "A company-specific brain built for how your business actually runs."
- Description of small, specialized language models vs. general-purpose LLMs
- Google DeepMind case study quote block
- InstaWorkers™: "AI teammates that run on top of that brain."
- "They work inside the tools you already use." + icons for ERP, CRM, Inboxes, Pricing Systems, Service Systems

**Why This Matters (dark background):**
- "When intelligence is cheap and execution is native to your systems, work stops backing up."
- "And when work gets done, revenue moves."

**Audience Segments (light background):**
- Title: "Built for the teams that move the physical economy."
- Three segments with toggles/tabs: SALES TEAMS | OPERATIONS TEAMS | LEADERSHIP TEAMS
- Sales Teams: quote faster, follow up, suggest alternatives, cover territory, spend time selling
- Operations Teams: identify parts, resolve pricing/inventory, prep jobs, keep work moving
- Leadership Teams: institutional knowledge, scale without headcount, existing systems, measurable impact
- Each ends with "The result: [outcome]"

**Testimonials:**
- "Love Letters to InstaLILY" — warm branding
- Three quotes from: Sales Manager (National Distributor), VP of Operations (Field-Service Organization), CEO (Physical Goods Company)

**Final CTA:**
- "See what selling faster really looks like."
- "Hire InstaWorkers™" button

**Footer:**
- Features | Hire InstaWorkers™ | Team | News | Careers | AI Dispatch | Women in AI
- HELLO@INSTALILY.AI
- Privacy Policy | Terms of Service | Do Not Sell

### Design Observations
- Bold dark/light alternating sections
- Strong typographic hierarchy — very large statement text
- Monospace uppercase labels for section categories
- Full-bleed hero photography (real people, real work)
- Minimal product screenshots — concept-driven
- Cream/warm white for light sections, deep navy/black for dark sections
- Floating nav bar with rounded corners and subtle transparency
- Premium, confident brand voice
