# Landing Page Comparison: OPSA vs InstaLILY

## Executive Summary

Both OPSA and InstaLILY serve the physical goods / wholesale economy with AI-powered sales solutions. However, InstaLILY's landing page significantly outperforms OPSA's in storytelling, visual impact, and brand sophistication. This document outlines the key differences, incorporates insights from OPSA's customer and investor decks, and proposes a strategy for OPSA's rebrand.

---

## OPSA Company Profile (from Decks)

### Positioning
- **Investor framing:** "Agentic Commerce for B2B, Starting with Wholesale"
- **Customer framing:** "Your Round-the-Clock Sales Team That Never Stops Selling"
- **Core thesis:** ERP records transactions. It doesn't drive them. The next evolution is agentic: autonomous AI agents that perceive demand, decide what to sell, and execute transactions end-to-end.

### Traction & Metrics (use on landing page)
- **$200K ARR** — 3 paying design partners
- **$125K incremental monthly revenue** per partner (90-day average)
- **60% dormant lead re-engagement rate** within 3 months
- **40+ orders closed** by first customer alone in 90 days
- Revenue-share pricing: "Opsa only charges when it generates measurable incremental revenue"

### Key Pain Points to Highlight (from investor deck)
1. Sales lives in the inbox — quotes, pricing, negotiations buried in email and WhatsApp
2. Selling doesn't scale — reps bottleneck when demand spikes
3. Revenue driven by memory — upsells rely on tribal knowledge
4. Margin leaks at every step — pricing rules applied inconsistently
5. Reps spend 60% of time on admin, not selling

### Why Now (from investor deck — strong landing page narrative)
1. **LLMs** can now read, understand, and respond to wholesale emails with human-level comprehension
2. **ERP API Access** — modern ERPs expose APIs; agents can query inventory, check margins, create orders in real time
3. **Email & Inbox Control** — agents can live natively inside email and WhatsApp, where selling already happens

### Product Architecture: Signal > Decide > Act > Learn
1. **Signal/Detect** — Email & WhatsApp signals, ERP inventory data, trade show leads
2. **Decide/Rank** — Best offers by margin, buyer purchase history, price sensitivity
3. **Act/Execute** — Send personalized pricing, negotiate within guardrails, create sales orders
4. **Learn/Improve** — Learn from every outcome, sharpen buyer profiles, compound intelligence

### Three Orchestration Modes
- **Inbound Autonomy (Full Auto)** — Repeat orders from returning customers, fully automatic
- **Autonomous Full Cycle (AI-Led)** — Known buyers, AI negotiates within pre-set limits
- **Supervised Orchestration (Human-in-Loop)** — New/high-value orders, human greenlights

### 5-Step How It Works (from customer deck)
1. Connect Your Data — ERP, leads, and inventory in one place
2. Build Buyer Profiles — AI profiles every buyer account
3. Launch Smart Outreach — Right buyer, right offer, right time
4. Handle Replies — AI responds; flags your team when ready
5. Close the Deal — Quotes sent, orders confirmed, team notified

Opsa is the only platform that is simultaneously:

## 1. Hero Section

### OPSA (Current)
- **Headline:** "Unlock AI-Driven Incremental Revenue for Wholesalers & Manufacturers"
- **Subtext:** Describes AI-powered sales assistants for quotes, order tracking, customer inquiries
- **CTA:** "Talk to founders" (dark rounded button)
- **Visual:** None — plain light background with a small green "Automate Revenue!" badge
- **Stats bar:** "15-20% revenue increase / Instant quote generation / Hassle-free setup"

### InstaLILY
- **Headline:** "Don't slow down. Sell faster."
- **Subtext:** "AI that turns complex work into revenue. Built for teams in the physical economy."
- **CTA:** "Hire InstaWorkers" (outlined button, sticky nav)
- **Visual:** Full-bleed overhead photo of people working with physical documents
- **Social proof banner:** "Featured in Google DeepMind" (persistent top bar)

### Analysis
InstaLILY's hero is dramatically more compelling. The short, punchy headline creates urgency. The full-bleed photography grounds the product in the real world. OPSA's hero is wordy and lacks visual impact.

### Recommendation
- **New headline options** (from deck language):
  - "Your Sales Team That Never Sleeps"
  - "From Inbox to Invoice. Automatically."
  - "AI That Sells. You Close."
  - "Stop Chasing Quotes. Start Closing."
- Use the **$125K/mo incremental revenue** stat as the hero proof point
- Add a credibility banner: "Trusted by wholesale distributors generating $125K+ in monthly incremental revenue"
- Full-bleed warehouse/distribution imagery

2. **DECIDE** — Rank best offers by margin, buyer history, price sensitivity

- **Full Auto** — Repeat orders, zero human touch
- **AI-Led** — Known buyers, AI negotiates within limits
- **Human-in-Loop** — New/high-value deals, human greenlights

This gives visitors an immediate understanding of how Opsa fits different deal types.
